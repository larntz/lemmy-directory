Description

Wecome to the aesthetic community of solarpunk, where everything that reminds you of it has a place, be it visual, audible, fashion or anything else!

Remember to follow the instance rules when interacting, and also:

-Cite the author whenever possible, or state it as unknown when unsure.

-We have a sister community where solarpunk artwork is posted, [/c/art](https://slrpnk.net/c/art), so even though art is also welcome here, keep it in mind when posting.

-Keep it SFW.

Hope you enjoy your time here! :D

As a last thing, kindly reminder that solarpunk is not *just* a form of artwork/aesthetic, but also a mindset and a movemet. For more on this, you can check our community [/c/solarpunk](https://slrpnk.net/c/solarpunk).
