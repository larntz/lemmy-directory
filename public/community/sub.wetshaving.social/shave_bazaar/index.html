Description

1. Tag your post in the title. The approved tags are as follows:

[WTS] Want to Sell
[WTB] Want to Buy
[WTT] Want to Trade
[WTG] Want to Give
[WTS/WTT] Want to Sell or Trade
[Vendor] Provider of goods and services through their own site
[Service] Honing, restoration, sensual massage...
[Artisan] You make your own goods by hand and you think you're fancy
[Hobby] Not quite a vendor or service, but more than a casual trader

2. Provide an accurate description and pricing
All sale posts must include a price but it may be negotiable.
Auction style pricing will not be permitted.
An accurate description of the item should be provided.
Pictures of the item are highly encouraged for accuracy.

3. Be courteous and respectful adults.

4. No link only posts. Link posts or posts with just a link in the text box will be deleted. Please include some other description or information in the post.

5. Only list items you own/provide. Do not link to eBay auctions, deals you found elsewhere, sales on other forums or items that aren't yours.

6. 1 post per tag per day. There is a maximum of 3 posts per day, each must be of separate types like [WTS], [WTT] or [WTB] for example.

No more than 5 total posts of all types within a week maximum.

Vendors may post once per week.

7. Wait at least 7 days to repost items. Wait at least 7 days to repost an item. This includes deleting and reposting before 7 days.

There is a 'WTS Reduced' flair that can be used to draw attention if you've edited to reduce prices.

8. Update your post's flair and mark NSFW. Update the link flair on your post to NSFW once all sales/trades are final. Editing post flair should be available on all platforms.

This should be done for all [WTS], [WTT], [WTG] and even [WTB] posts. This helps keep our community clean and lets others know if items are unavailable or if someone is still looking to buy.

9. Do not delete prices or posts/comments that have sold items. Posts or comments that include sold items, along with their prices, should be left so that buyers can search item price history as well as seller reliability.

10. Use at your own risk. The moderators and administrators are not responsible for any transactions in any way.
