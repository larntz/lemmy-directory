Description

Welcome to 988, a Lemmy community!

988 is a community dedicated to providing emotional support, encouragement, and understanding to individuals experiencing mental health challenges, personal struggles, and emotional difficulties. We are here to foster a compassionate environment for personal growth and support.

Community Rules:

Stay on Topic: Our primary focus is mental health, personal growth, and emotional support. Please keep discussions related to these topics.
Use Appropriate Language: Respectful and constructive language is required at all times. Abusive or offensive language will not be tolerated.
Flair Your Posts: If possible, please use flairs to help others understand the context of your post quickly.
Be Mindful of Triggers: Content that could potentially trigger emotional distress should be clearly marked as such.
No Spam or Self-Promotion: Posts and comments should contribute to the conversation and community. Self-promotion and spam are not permitted.
Protect Privacy: Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information without explicit consent.
Seek Professional Help When Needed: While this community provides support, it does not replace professional help. If you are in crisis or need professional help, please reach out to appropriate services in your area.
Feel free to reach out to our mod team or community members if you need assistance or have any questions.

Remember, we're all here to help each other. Let's keep this community a safe, open, and understanding place for everyone.
