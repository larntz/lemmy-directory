# This community is for discussing what goes on in the so-called dark web/darknet.
If this community goes well, it may remain open. If things get out of hand and moderation is too difficult, I may have to close it.

### RULES

Because of the sensitive nature of the darkweb, there is a fine line between acceptable discussion and criminality.

- Be respectful of others. No bigotry, discrimination, harassment or personal attacks. Argue the merit of points and ideas, not against the person themselves.
- No sourcing.
- No reviews or complaints. This is not for customer support or product/service reviews.
- No encouraging people to buy stuff or commit crimes, such as recommending a vendor or market.
- No advertising/shilling of markets, products or vendors. No referral links/codes.
- No discussion of politics.
- No discussion of carding or money laundering.
- No pornographic or gore images. EVER.
- Follow your local laws and regulations, as well as the rules of your instance.

These rules may change at any time. It is *_your_* responsibility to know and follow them at all times. Failure to do so puts you at risk of a ban.
