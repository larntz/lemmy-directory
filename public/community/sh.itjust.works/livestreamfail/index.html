Welcome to /c/LivestreamFail: the place for almost anything livestream related. No moderators of this subreddit work for Twitch or any streaming platforms.

# Rules

1. **Be respectful:** We want this community to be a fun and welcoming place for everyone. Please be respectful to your fellow community members and avoid personal attacks, hate speech, or any other form of discrimination.

2. **Stay on topic:** This community is dedicated to live streaming fails and related content. Please keep your posts and comments relevant to the topic at hand.

3. **No self-promotion:** While we encourage sharing content from other creators, please refrain from promoting your own content or channels. This includes links to your own streams or social media profiles.

4. **No reposts:** Before posting, please check to make sure that the content you're sharing hasn't already been posted. Duplicate posts will be removed.
