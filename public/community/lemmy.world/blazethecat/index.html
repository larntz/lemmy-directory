Description

This is a communtiy dedicated to Blaze The Cat. Pretty self-explanatory.

Rules:

* 1. Be respectful.
Personal attacks and hurtful insults against others will not be tolerated. Remember to remain civil.

* 2. Flair your posts correctly.
Use the Flair option when posting so users can sort accordingly, so [artwork], [mod], [video], etc,

* 3. Don't post porn or other NSFW content.
Porn can include, but is not limited to, videos, images, gifs, smut, audio files, snippets of the aforementioned type of content that are not inherently pornographic (such as Bench Tails), and suggestive, sexual, or lewd posts.

* 4. Keep posts relevant to Blaze the Cat.
Your post was removed because it was not related to Blaze the Cat.

* 5. Bigotry of any kind will not be tolerated.
This includes, but is not limited to, racism, homophobia, transphobia, ableism, misogyny, and xenophobia.

* 6. Don't post content that may make others uncomfortable.
Before you post, ask yourself if a reasonable person would be offended or made uncomfortable by your content. Such content may include, but is not limited to, posts with excessive blood/gore, pornography, Nazi imagery, and extremely dark humor.

* 7. Credit the Original Artist/Creator
Enter the name of the artist in the title of your post. If you don't know the artist's username, simply say something like Artist unknown. If possible, also include the link to the artwork from the creator's social media account or website in the comments.

* 8. Shipshaming is forbidden.
You can critique ships, but you are not allowed to personally attack others for enjoying particular ships.

* 9. Do not post low-effort or low-quality content.
Please message a moderator if you are unsure if your post would be deemed low-effort or low-quality. This is subjective and open to interpretation, but you may also appeal if you disagree with a post removal.

* 10. Do not spam.

* 11. External Link Policy
Only post links to secure and verified websites. Links to cloud storage archives, such as Dropbox and Google Drive, and media file sharing websites, such as Mediafire, are prohibited. If you are sharing non-pirated game assets for modding, GameBanana and GitHub are acceptable sources.
