Description

A place for you to post about tarantulas!

Allowed content:

Questions regarding tarantula care.
Tarantula pictures.
Help identifying a tarantula.
Tarantula memes.
Enclosure setups and questions.
Anything else related to tarantulas!

Rules:

Content containing dangerous care or any post where the tarantula's life may be in danger due to owner negligence or mishandling will not be tolerated and will be removed. This includes pictures where a tarantula may be susceptible to a fall because you're holding it high above the floor. **Don't put your pets in danger for your own enjoyment, tarantulas get nothing out of being held.**

While it's encouraged to help people whose enclosures aren't suitable for a tarantula to live happily in, don't be rude when offering help or criticism.

That's about it! Enjoy your tarantulas.
