# Rules

1) Submissions must directly relate to Nickelodeon All-Star Brawl.

2) Use search (No reposts or commonly asked questions within 1 month).

3) Be respectful. Toxicity Will NOT be tolerated!

4) Monetized content/self-promotion.

5) Relating to harmful game mods and cheating.

6) Art submissions must directly relate to the game (No general Nickelodeon art).

7) Do not create spam/repetitive posts/comments.

8) Do not share explicit/violent content.
