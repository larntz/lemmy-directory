Rules
---
1. Only post art created under a Creative Commons or other permissive license.
2. Include the license in the title in brackets (such as `[CC-BY]`, `[CC-BY-SA-4.0]`) -- all posts must have a license associated with them.
3. NSFW is allowed, but MUST BE posted as NSFW!
