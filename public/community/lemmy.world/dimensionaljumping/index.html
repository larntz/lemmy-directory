### INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the Kbin Dimensional Jumping magazine. This place exists to provide a new avenue for Reddit migrators to discuss the phenomena of dimensional shifts, leaps, jumps, etc. Copied from the original subreddit, [t]his is a place to share your personal experiences of the shifting nature of reality, through the deliberate application of techniques to bring about 'jumps' in our personal worlds - in effect, switching to a more desirable universe.

Universe # 982

Please note that a shift in your experience does not require a change in [this] number, which should be treated as an emblem of change and a symbol of potential rather than an ID.

### OVERVIEW

The essence of Dimensional Jumping is the leveraging of perspective-structuring metaphors and the techniques derived from them. Links to the original methods are archived on the original subreddit and will be duplicated here as some of the introductory posts. Among them are the Mirror Method, Active Metaphors (Hall of Records, Infinite Grid, etc), and the Owls of Eternity and Two Glass experiments.

### RULES

The rules are pretty straightforward.

1. Be good to each other. This should go without saying but please post quality content, treat other users with respect, and above all else just be kind. Try to be open to others experiences and don't limit your perspective by engaging in bad conduct. It's possible that the other user may not understand the material at first--every new interaction is a learning opportunity.

2. This is not the community for fictional horror stories/tales. Please only post genuine experiences, experiments, trials, questions, etc.

3. As a result of Rule #2, please assume that the stories you read here are truth and not falsehood.

4. Don't spam.
