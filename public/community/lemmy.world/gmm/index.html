An unofficial Lemmy community for Rhett and Link's [Good Mythical Morning](https://www.youtube.com/user/RhettandLink2) channel.

#### Rules
1. **Be Your Mythical Best:** No hateful, racist, sexist, or otherwise discriminatory contributions or comments. Be kind to each other, or at least courteous.
2. **Stay on Topic:** Keep it about GMM, Rhett and Link, or one Rhett and Link's other projects.
3. **Keep it (mostly) SFW:** With the exception of the few GMM episodes featuring NSFW content, keep it SFW. Flag all posts containing discussion of GMM's rare NSFW content as NSFW.
4. **Keep the Mythical Society mythical:** Don't share Mythical Society leaks.
