# FAQ

**Q: What is dcss?**
*Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup is an open-source roguelike game where players explore a randomized dungeon, facing monsters, traps, and treasures. It offers a wide range of races, classes, and gods, each with unique abilities. The game emphasizes tactical gameplay, resource management, and strategic decision-making. With challenging gameplay and permadeath, each playthrough is different due to procedural generation. The community actively contributes to the game's development, making it available for free on multiple platforms.*
