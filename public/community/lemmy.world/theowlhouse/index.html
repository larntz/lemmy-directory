Welcome to the Owl House Community!

Join us in celebrating and discussing the spectacular animated fantasy television series, *The Owl House*, created by Dana Terrace and her team at Disney TV Animation.

At our vibrant and inclusive community, we aim to reflect the spirit of the show we love. Here, diversity and creativity flourish, fostering an environment where everyone can freely express themselves.

***Community Guidelines***

**Respect and Kindness:**

Treat every member of our community with respect and kindness. Avoid making mean, rude, or harassing comments or posts. Remember, we are here to celebrate and enjoy The Owl House together.

**No Abusive Language:**

The use of derogatory language or personal attacks directed towards any other users is strictly prohibited. We have a zero-tolerance policy for any form of bigotry. Violations will result in an immediate ban.

**No Spamming:**

Invading our community with unsolicited advertisements or spam messages is not allowed. Let's keep the focus on sharing and discussing The Owl House in a meaningful way.

**No Pornographic or NSFW Material:**

Our community is intended to be a safe space for everyone. Please refrain from sharing any pornographic or NSFW (Not Safe for Work) content. We encourage you to share art, videos, and creative material that aligns with the spirit of The Owl House.

**Properly Credit Artists:**

When sharing artwork, always give credit to the original artist. Include the artist's name or pseudonym in the post title, along with a link to their work in the body of your post. Respecting and appreciating the creators behind the art is essential.
