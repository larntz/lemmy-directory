### Rules of Conduct:
(shamelessly lifted from the subreddit, but more lenient)

::: spoiler 1) General Post Content
This is an Anaheim Ducks centric sub and also a hockey sub.

You are free to post on topics that are directly or indirectly related to the Anaheim Ducks. The mods are lenient, in that we will allow posts related to Anaheim Ducks Alumni, the Honda Center, or attending games and other similar topics.

When in doubt about the contents of your post it is better to avoid posting.

Note: POSTS WHICH ARE NOT DIRECTLY RELATED TO THE TEAM MAY BE SUBJECT TO REMOVAL AT MOD DISCRETION.
:::
::: spoiler 2) Post Topics to Avoid
In addition to our rules on General Post Content, posts directly or indirectly relating to any of the following are not allowed:

Hate Speech or Defamatory Statements.

Note: Inappropriate comments within posts directed at other users or the subject of the post itself are subject to the guidelines above. A failure to comply with the guidelines or abuse of the rules will result in the mods taking action against users.
:::
::: spoiler 3) Trolling
Don't be an asshole. Don't start shit in other subs with other fans post-game or with other subs in our threads. Its a simple rule that is ubiquitous throughout ~~hockey reddit~~ life. Enough said.
:::
