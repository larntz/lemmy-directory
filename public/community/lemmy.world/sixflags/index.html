**c/sixflags Rules**

- Tickets!

Selling of tickets online may result in a scam. Always buy from authorized sellers or buy at your own risk. Tickets are way too expensive to be scammed.

- Be Nice!

Stay respectful, polite, and friendly. Don't insult people or their good intentions in a post, comment, or otherwise, even if a person seems rude or ill-informed.

Use of bigoted slurs, directed at other users or not, will be removed and possibly banned.

- Advertising

Advertising will not be permitted without first contacting mods for approval.

This applies to advertising other subs, advertising products and so on.
