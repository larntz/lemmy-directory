# Rules

**1. Be nice**

Be respectful and constructive. No harassment. No grossly offensive images. No hate including racism, misogyny, transphobia, ableism, etc.

**2. Stay on topic**

All top-level posts to the community must be a link to a video essay that isn't hosted behind a pay wall. Any subject (that doesn't break another rule) is welcome. Include the title of the video and it's duration in the format (MM:SS) or (HH:MM:SS) in the post title.

**3. No shitposts**

Noticeably low-quality content and videos that really stretch a reasonable definition of video essay will be removed.
