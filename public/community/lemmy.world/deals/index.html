Description

The home for Deals on Lemmy

Post deals and sales for any items.

1. Prohibited Postings: No firearms, ammunition, or explosives. No drugs, including alcohol, CBD, tobacco, controlled substances. No counterfeit/generic/unbranded/knock-off merchandise. No gambling/sports betting. No cryptocurrency. No pirated software or fraud in general. Read why here

2. Posts must directly link to offers.

3. No drop-shipping/Shopify stores/self-promotion/Kickstarters/user-to-user sales/PM me for details.

Although self-promotion can lead to great deals for users, the likelihood that the deals aren't true deals is reason enough to exclude them. Also, this is not Craigslist and you can't take advantage of people with your Shopify store.

5. No contests/chance-to-win/giveaways that charge s&h/auctions/Discord or Facebook-type groups.

eBay Buy It Now purchases are permitted only if the seller is a nationally recognized business (ie TigerDirect) or has a significant user rating, ie 50,000+ sales with a 90%+ rating. There must also be a large available quantity, ideally 99+.

TEMPORARILY BANNABLE

6. If there is a time parameter for the deal list that in the title as well. E.g. [Ends 5.13.18].

7. No 3rd-party sellers.

Deals must be shipped/sold directly by the Website company, i.e. Amazon, Target, etc. An exception will be made if the 3rd-party is selling the product cheaper than if buying it directly from the parent Website.

8. Sale price must be included in the title.

Unless it is a generic site-wide coupon (i.e. 30% off at Kohls), the price must be included in the post title. If the sale is for a product line, the price range must be included (i.e. Nike shoes on sale $40-$60).
