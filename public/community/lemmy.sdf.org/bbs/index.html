Find one of hundreds of BBSes running **today** using the lists found at [The Telnet BBS Guide](http://www.telnetbbsguide.com/), [BBS Guide](http://bbs.guide/), or [IPTIA BBS Resource Site](https://www.ipingthereforeiam.com/bbs/).

You will want to use a proper BBS compatible terminal to get the full experience: [SyncTERM](http://syncterm.bbsdev.net) ^(win,mac,lin), [Netrunner](http://mysticbbs.com/downloads.html) ^(win/lin), or [mTelnet](http://mt32.bbses.info/) ^(win,os2)

---

Find oodles of software, text files, and people at [The BBS Archives](http://archives.thebbs.org/) and [The BBS Corner](http://www.bbscorner.com/).
