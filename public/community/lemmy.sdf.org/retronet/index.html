Description

Community for discussing internet and software topics that are 20+ years old.

Topics can be sites/fads/software/games/memes or any general happenings that used to occur on computers way back in the day.

Submission Guidelines:

1) Topic of discussion should have originated 20+ years ago. A topic (such as a fad or web game) that originated 20 years ago but remained popular for a couple years is ok, as long as it first began 20+ years ago.

2) Topic should be software or internet focused. For retro hardware discussion try [!retrocomputing@lemmy.sdf.org](https://lemmy.sdf.org/c/retrocomputing)

3) Topics on standalone software and operating systems are also allowed: Windows 3.1, Winamp, etc.
