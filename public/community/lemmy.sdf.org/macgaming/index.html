Welcome to the Mac Gaming Community!

This is a place for all Mac gamers to discuss, share, and enjoy the world of gaming on macOS. Whether you're a casual player or a dedicated enthusiast, we welcome you to join our community and share your passion for Mac gaming!
Rules

Be respectful and civil. Treat others how you'd like to be treated. Personal attacks, harassment, and hate speech will not be tolerated.
Stay on topic. All posts and discussions should be related to Mac gaming. Off-topic content may be removed.
No piracy or illegal content. Do not share or promote pirated games, software, or any other illegal content.
No self-promotion or spam. Keep the community clean by not sharing your own or others' videos, streams, or websites excessively.
Use spoiler tags when necessary. Be considerate and use spoiler tags for content that could ruin the experience for others.
Follow Lemmy's guidelines. Abide by the Lemmy Community Guidelines at all times.
