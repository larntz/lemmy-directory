#### Standard format discussion**

Standard is a dynamic format where you build decks and play using cards in your collection from recently released\u202fMagic\u202fsets (about the last 3 years). Evolving gameplay and rotating strategies make it a fun and fresh way to play\u202fMagic.

Find out what sets are [currently in standard](https://whatsinstandard.com/).

Read more at [Standard's official website](https://magic.wizards.com/en/formats/standard).
