**Vintage format (Type 1) discussion.**

Play your moxen here! Vintage allows for cards to be played from *all* Magic card sets and includes cards from expansions and special sets! In addition to its banned list, Vintage uniquely holds a restricted list for the super broken cards, which limits the use of a card to a single copy.

Read more at [Vintage's official website](https://magic.wizards.com/en/formats/vintage).
