#General Rules:
1. No NSFW content of any kind is permitted.
1. Submissions should be related to Tom, the comic, or the subreddit. You should be able to tell it's related without reading the title. This is done case by case.
1. Respect each other, don't be a dick.
1. Nov 13th is [Katurday](http://www.gunnerkrigg.com/?p=1909).
1. Do not spoil anything in the ongoing chapter as we have a number of readers who only read on chapter end. This means no spoilers in titles and posts about the current chapter must be spoiler marked.
