Description

This subreddit was created to act as a repository for packet captures & other legally shareable online game data pertaining to games that are now defunct/offline (or soon to be). The purpose of this subreddit is to offer a place to discuss & share important online game data that is not illegal in nature.
The ultimate goal is to preserve as much of online gaming history as possible and act as a centralized location for the allocation of such data for future engineering/emulation.

Originally [r/OnlinePreservation](https://www.reddit.com/r/OnlinePreservation) on reddit.
