Description

A software engineering community for those who have been in the industry a while, and would like to discuss topics beyond the beginner/intermediate level.

This might include, but not limited to: soft skills e.g. leading teams, working effectively as an individual contributor, techniques/advice for career progression, conflict resolution, interviewing, or mental health. It might also include discussion of hard skills, such as architectural designs, new high-impact technologies or tools, blog posts, war stories, sharing of projects you want help with or just want to show off, even embarrassing dumb questions that you think you should know as the incredible experienced dev you are :)
