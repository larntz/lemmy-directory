# FlightGear Republic

We are a respectful and inclusive community of aviation and flight simulation enthusiasts committed to growth and collaboration.

We use [FlightGear](https://www.flightgear.org/), an Open Source Flight Simulation software, for our Flight Simulation needs and have fun.

## About

This is a Community for FlightGear. Where Freedom and Respect meet. Share your thoughts freely and discuss Free Flight Simulation.

You can interact with this community from any federated Lemmy instance, just search for [`!flightgearrepublic@lemmy.ml`](https://lemmy.ml/c/flightgearrepublic)

### ðŸŽ– Code Of Honour
1. We respect all members of the FlightGear community, regardless of their background, beliefs, or opinions, and we create a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who shares our passion for flight simulation.
2. We value honesty and integrity in all our interactions, and we do not condone cheating or any other form of dishonesty that undermines the realism and fairness of flight simulation.
3. We strive to improve our skills as virtual pilots and developers, and we encourage training, practice, and knowledge sharing to enhance the realism and accuracy of our simulations.
4. We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork, and we work together on projects and initiatives that promote the growth and development of the flight simulation community.
5. We celebrate diversity and inclusivity and reject discrimination, harassment, or any other form of disrespectful behaviour that detracts from the enjoyment and learning opportunities of flight simulation.
6. We are open to constructive feedback and criticism, and we use it as an opportunity to learn and improve ourselves and our simulations.
7. We respect the intellectual property rights of others, and we do not use or distribute copyrighted material without proper authorization or attribution.
8. We are committed to contributing to the FlightGear project and the flight simulation community as a whole, and we recognize the importance of giving back to the community that has provided us with this incredible platform for learning and exploration.
9. We value personal growth and development, and we encourage members to challenge themselves, set goals, and strive for excellence in their virtual piloting and development skills.
10. We are proud to be members of the FlightGear Republic community and represent it with honour and dignity both online and offline, as ambassadors of the flight simulation community.
