Description

A community for fans of the Shingeki no Kyojin (Attack on Titan) manga and anime, written by Hajime Isayama and produced by Studio WiT (seasons 1-3) and MAPPA (season 4).

**Rules:**
- No unmarked spoilers! You must mark them with Lemmy's built-in spoiler tag and indicate in
*both the post title and the spoiler title* what they pertain to (Manga chapters which haven't been adapted yet, Anime episodes, etc.), as such:

::: spoiler [Manga Spoilers]
Made you look! ;)
:::

- Additionally, if the post contains an image, you must either include it in the body behind a spoiler tag or mark the post as NSFW, otherwise it will be shown by default to users. Unmarked spoilers will be removed.
- No spoilers in the title itself are allowed.
- Be civil, you must adhere to Lemmy's Code of Conduct at all times.
- If you post fan artwork which you haven't made, you must *always* credit the original author, either by linking to the original author's page directly or by stating it in a comment.
- Sharing or endorsing unofficial/pirated distributions of the manga/anime is not allowed. We should support the official release. Note that sharing chapter leaks before the chapter is released internationally *is* allowed for the sake of discussion, but this can only be done in the comments of discussion threads and should be removed after the chapter officially releases.
