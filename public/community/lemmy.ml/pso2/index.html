# What is Phantasy Star Online 2?
Phantasy Star Online 2 (PSO2) is a online free-to-play action RPG developed and published by SEGA. Originally releasing in 2012, it later got a expansion titled Phantasy Star Online 2: New Genesis (PSO2:NGS) in 2021, which is essentially its own game.

Players can easilly jump between NGS and Classic PSO2 and play each with the same character. Each has their own story, quests and content to play.

PSO2:NGS is avaliable as Global (PC, PS4, Xbox One) and Japanese (PC, PS4 and Switch Cloud) releases. The Global and Japanese servers are not linked, and the Japanese servers require that you live in Japan to play on them.
