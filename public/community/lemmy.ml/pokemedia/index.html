Rules:

1. All posts should be social media in the Pokemon universe.

Think creatively on what the citizens or important figures of the Pokemon world would post in their free time. Note that some level of canon divergence is allowed.

Image posts only please.

2. All spoilers should be marked as such.

Whenever the new games come out, posts relating to them must use the Spoiler Flair for at least a month after their release.

3. No Recent/Top Reposts

No reposts within 2 months (we may shorten this, we'll see how many posts we start to get) or from our top 10 of all time.

4. Be civil

Be civil and treat other people nicely. Bigotry and harassment will not be tolerated.
Refrain from creating posts that specifically target and shoot down other people's posts.

5. Use /uj when not roleplaying.

Taken straight from circlejerk subs/uj (unjerk) and /rj (rejerk) represent whether you are roleplaying or not.
Unjerk means your comment is serious.
Rejerk is not required unless there's also a /uj in your comment and you want to differentiate between the two.

6. Tag your posts

The Reddit version of this community uses Post Flairs to distinguish different genres of posts. Since Lemmy does not currently have Post Flairs, we instead ask you to tag your posts in the title, e.g. Look what my Poochyena did! [Casual], Crazy Adventure Part 3 [Storyline], etc.
