This page is for sharing and discussing farm designs created in Stardew Valley.

---

**Rules:**

1. *Keep discussion to farm design*

Posts should be focused on sharing or discussing farm layouts. General questions and other aspects of the game can be discussed on [/c/StardewValley](https://lemmy.ml/c/stardewvalley). House and Town decorations are also allowed but all posts show generally focus on layouts.

2. *Please credit the farm's creator*

If you are posting someone else's farm, you must credit the original creator by linking to the original reddit post or upload.farm page.

3. *Keep language friendly*

It's only a game. Hostile language towards others is not appropriate.
