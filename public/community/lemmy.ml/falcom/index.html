# Rules

- Follow Lemmy.ml's rules and [Code of Conduct](https://join-lemmy.org/docs/en/code_of_conduct.html).
- **Be respectful.**
- **No NSFW content.**
*Pornographic content is against Lemmy.ml's rules.*
- **Properly tag spoilers.**
*Thanks to the delay in the localization of Falcom's games, as well as for the consideration of new fans, please consider if what you are posting could be a spoiler for someone who has not yet played the game you are discussing. This is especially important for story-related items. Lemmy doesn't currently allow you to spoiler tag a post, so please do not put spoilers in your title.*
- **Properly Source Fanart.**
*If you are not linking directly to the artwork's source (which is preferred) you must provide a link to the source in the comments.*
- **No Illegal Content**
*Please do not link to any unofficial means of obtaining official content, such as games.*
