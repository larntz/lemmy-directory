### A community created to bring together like-minded creatives for the purpose of collaboration, networking, and gaining experience. Keep your topic(s) SFW so that recruiters and those at an office have nothing to worry themselves over while browsing.

### Rules:

* All post titles must include if [Hobby], [RevShare], [Paid], [ForHire] or [ComesFrom].

\t[Hobby] Your title here

\tJust learning or developing for fun?

\t[RevShare] Your title here

\tAre you recruiting for a project that doesn't pay up front?

\t[Paid] Your title here

\tAre you hiring for a position that pays?
