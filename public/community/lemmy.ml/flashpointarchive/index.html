The official Lemmy community of BlueMaxima's Flashpoint!

Flashpoint is an archive of webgames and interactives built on technologies such as Flash, HTML, Unity, and many others.

We do not archive games published by Nitrome, as they have specifically requested we not include their games in the archive. To engage with their community and see their progress in porting games to HTML5, check out their community [here](https://lemmy.ml/c/nitrome).
