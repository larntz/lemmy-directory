Some song *fragments* are just amazing. Share them here!

Even though there are songs that are amazing all the way through, sadly, **this community focuses on fragments**. If you have a song that's dank all the way, you could find a particular fragment to share here, or you could share the whole song [here](https://lemmy.ml/c/music)!

**Guidelines:**

**EITHER** share a link with the exact dank fragment (perhaps a video link that starts at the precise time)

**OR** share the whole song (the name of the song or link to it) and tell us in your post what/where the dank fragment is!

Share your dankness!
