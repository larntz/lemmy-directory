**WELCOME!**

This is the hub for all things Adobe Illustrator. This group is for general discussion, showing off your designs, questions, tricks, techniques, troubleshooting and so forth. Give by helping others learn and improve their skills, critique and receive critique on the latest user designs, or simply sit back and enjoy casual discussion Illustrator related!

**WHAT WE'RE ABOUT**

This community is meant to strive in kindness and helping others learn and grow. Most design subs don't allow critique and new logo posts, but we do. As long as it was made in Illustrator, your post may spring up discussion or inspiration to another user. Please do not flame/harass another user; such activity may result in banning. Only downvote off-topic posts/comments; this helps with creating positive community morale.
