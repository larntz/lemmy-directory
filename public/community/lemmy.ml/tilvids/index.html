**Welcome to TILvids**

[TILvids](https://tilvids.com) is a video community focused on edutainment content. The goal is to deliver interesting videos that you might not otherwise see. Some other important information:

- We do not have video ads, but are [funded by donations](https://www.patreon.com/tilvids).
- We put a heavy emphasis on respecting the data privacy of our users, and building a healthy relationship with content creators.
- The site runs on the fantastic open-source [PeerTube](https://joinpeertube.org/) ecosystem.
- Feel free to join our community to receive daily TILvids video of the day posts, or to ask questions and contribute!

**Other Information**

- Visit [tilvids.com](https://tilvids.com) to watch great videos!
- Do you love Mastodon? [So do we](https://mstdn.social/@tilvids)!
- We're on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/tilvids) as well.

- Lots of additional information on our [About page](https://tilvids.com/about/instance).
