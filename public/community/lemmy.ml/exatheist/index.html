Description

This is a virtual meetinghouse for those who were once atheists, but have since found (or returned) to a different religion/philosophical orientation (including agnosticism or Deism) All are welcome here! Feel free to post links of interest, or topics for discussion.

Please be courteous and civilized here, in this place where ex-atheists from all walks of life can meet.

We understand as well as anybody else that there will be disagreements between our respective religions and philosophical positions, but please remember to be courteous. We're all siblings, as ex-atheists, and as human beings.

Due to the highly niche nature of the community, the strict rules in place, and the fact that our readers are often busy in their own respective communities, this is a slow-moving community. Please bear with us. Commenting in less-than-recent threads is OK.

Rules

This is for the community and benefit of ex-atheists.

Please communicate with respect and open-mindedness. Insults and slurs will not be tolerated.

No proselytizing.

THIS IS NOT A DEBATE COMMUNITY.

OPs must provide a top-level comment with the summary and a discussion prompt of the content when they post the following sources: Pictures, videos, links, and crossposts. Posts that lack a top-level comment with a summary of the content and a discussion prompt will be removed.

Political discussion must be civilized and relevant to ex-atheism.

Atheists are welcome here. You are welcome to have conversations, not to proselytize or debate.


TROLLING WILL NOT BE TOLERATED.
