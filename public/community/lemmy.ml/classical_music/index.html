Description

A community for discussing what is widely dubbed _classical_ music, which is put simply, compositions for orchestral instruments, _as well as_ historical music on more traditional instruments.

Suitable examples:
* [_Sumer is icumen in_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sumer_is_icumen_in), a 13th century choral composition from Medieval England
* [Koto Music (album)](http://www.komuso.com/albums/albums.pl?album=350) by Kimio Eto (1959)
* [Symphony no. 2: _Mysterious Mountain_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Symphony_No._2_(Hovhaness)) by Alan Hovhaness (1955)

Unsuitable:
* [_Nightswimming_](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nightswimming) by R.E.M.

Film soundtracks will be allowed _for now_. However, it would be best to create another community for such posts.
