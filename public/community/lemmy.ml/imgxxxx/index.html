Digital cameras, camcorders, and other video recording devices save their videos to standard naming formats. Often, these videos are either automatically uploaded, or manually uploaded to sites like Youtube, without any change in the title.

They are often personal videos, instructional videos, or other such content that may be intended for specific viewers.

Type IMG, MVI or MOV and 4 digits of your choice into Youtube, Vimeo, DailyMotion, or other large video websites.
