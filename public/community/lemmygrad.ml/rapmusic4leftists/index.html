Description

Community for those sharing rap music with leftist perspectives. All rap styles are welcome!

Rules:

1. Follow site rules
2. No racism, bigotry, ableism, etc. No rappers that fall into one of these categories.
3. No rap music promoting capitalism, fascism, imperialism, etc. No promoting capitalist rappers, and no music promoting Gucci bags, exotic cars, etc.
4. Titles must be in (Artist name - Song name) or vice versa format. If you don't know the artist, you may put Unknown.
5. No misogynistic and misandrist music. Empowerment does not mean hating each other.
6. Rappers promoting hate towards lower class such as using broke as an insult will be removed.
7. No Reactionary songs
8. No old heads. No prejudice against certain styles.
9. No reposts, and unrelated content will be removed.

Enjoy your stay here! Make friends, discuss leftist rappers, and find new songs to fill your playlists! Have fun and thanks for stopping by! :))
