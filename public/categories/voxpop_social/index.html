**Welcome to our NYC community!** Welcome to your go-to source for all things NYC! Whether you're a resident of the city that never sleeps, an out-of-towner planning a visit, or just someone who loves everything about the Big Apple, you've come to the right place. Here in VoxPop NYC, we're all about sharing and discussing our experiences, favorite spots, upcoming events, and the latest news in this vibrant and bustling city. **What we offer:** **Community:** Connect with fellow New Yorkers or NYC enthusiasts and build friendships with those who share your passion for this incredible city. **Information:** Find tips for the best restaurants, parks, tourist spots, hidden gems, or ask for advice on anything NYC-related. **Events:** Stay up-to-date with local events, gatherings, and festivals. **News:** Keep track of important city developments and local news. **Photos and Videos:** Share your favorite visuals of the city — from stunning skylines to snapshots of daily life. **Rules and Guidelines:** **Be Respectful:** This is a diverse community and we expect everyone to treat each other with kindness and respect. **Stay On Topic:** All posts must be directly related to NYC. **No Spam:** Spam, self-promotion, and unrelated links will not be tolerated. **Check Before You Post:** Use the search bar to make sure your question or topic hasn't been covered recently. **Report Problems:** If you see a problem, don't engage — report it to the mods. **Help Us Make VoxPop NYC Great:** This is your community, too! If you have any suggestions or ideas to improve VoxPop NYC, we're all ears. Together, we can make this the best place on VoxPop and Lemmy for all things NYC.
