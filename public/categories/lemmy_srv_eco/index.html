☀️ Solarpunk
Friday, June 16, 2023

Solarpunk is a literary and artistic movement that envisions and works toward actualizing a [sustainable future](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sustainable_development) interconnected with nature and community. The solar represents [solar energy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solar_energy) as a [renewable energy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Renewable_energy) source and an [optimistic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Optimism) vision of the future that rejects [climate doomerism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Doomer), while the punk refers to the [countercultural](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Counterculture), [post-capitalist](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Post-capitalism), and [decolonial](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decoloniality) enthusiasm for creating such a future. *~ from [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solarpunk)*
