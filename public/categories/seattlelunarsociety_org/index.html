Jokes You Can Tell Your Mom
Friday, June 16, 2023

Clean jokes (or cleaned-up jokes) that your Mom would like. NO: - F-bombs - Heavy use of expletives - Racism - LGBTQ slurs Humor is universal and laughter should be shared by everyone, without excluding or belittling others. It doesn't have to be crass to be funny. Take the high road. Your Mom would be proud of you. :) Dad jokes are OK here too, and you can find more of those here: https://lemmy.world/c/dadjokes
