Be diplomatic.** Participate in a courteous, objective, and open-minded fashion. Be nice to other posters and the people who make *Star Trek.* Disagree respectfully and don’t gatekeep. **4\. Tag spoilers.** Historically Daystrom has not had a spoiler policy, so you may encounter untagged spoilers here.
