Taken straight from circlejerk subs/uj (unjerk) and /rj (rejerk) represent whether you are roleplaying or not. Unjerk means your comment is serious. Rejerk is not required unless there's also a /uj in your comment and you want to differentiate between the two. 6. Tag your posts The Reddit version of this community uses Post Flairs to distinguish different genres of posts. [Miscellaneous]: Self-explanatory [PokeMedia Event]: Community-wide Events (ask Mods first) 7. No NSFW or extreme gore Yeah 8. Multiversal headcanon rules To avoid endless fighting over what is and isn't canon, every post is in its own universe, similar yet distinct from the rest, so if you have a specific headcanon that doesn't align with a post, we can chalk that inconsistency up to the multiverse.
