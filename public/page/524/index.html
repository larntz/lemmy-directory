[**Marathon Discord**](https://discord.com/invite/marathonthegame) --- ## **Rules:** - Be respectful: Treat fellow community members with respect and courtesy. Personal attacks, insults, or any form of harassment will not be tolerated. - Stay on-topic: Keep discussions related to Marathon and its related content, such as gameplay, story, mods, and community creations. Avoid unrelated topics or excessive self-promotion. - No spam or low-quality content: Refrain from posting repetitive or low-effort content. This includes low-quality screenshots, and unrelated or off-topic posts. Encourage meaningful discussions and contributions. - No spoilers without warning: To ensure everyone can enjoy the game and its story, mark any spoilers clearly and provide ample warning before discussing plot details or major twists. A lot of new people will be wanting play the classic games without knowing spoilers! - Encourage constructive discussions, upvote valuable contributions, and report any rule violations or suspicious activity to the moderators. - Engage in good faith: Encourage open-mindedness and constructive criticism. Be willing to engage in civil debates and discussions while respecting differing opinions. --- **Helpful Links:** [Aleph One](https://alephone.lhowon.org/) - Marathon is open source!
