Living in vans, cars, RVs, etc
Friday, June 16, 2023

We're not homeless, we're houseless! By choice or by circumstance we are living in our vehicles. Don't worry about us -- it can be a very good life. Anything that affects us _as vehicle-dwellers_ is probably on topic. # external resources - [emergency guide to suddenly living in a vehicle](https://rvwiki.mousetrap.net/doku.php?id=lifestyle:involuntary) <-- start here if you don't have a choice in the matter - [vanlife FAQ](https://rvwiki.mousetrap.net/doku.php?id=start#faq_for_newbies_and_the_van-curious) - [introduction to power in the vehicle](https://rvwiki.mousetrap.net/doku.php?id=electrical:12v:intro) - [gentle introduction to solar power](https://rvwiki.mousetrap.net/doku.php?id=electrical:solar:gentle_intro) - [overview of charging by alternator](https://rvwiki.mousetrap.net/doku.php?id=electrical:12v:alternator) - guides to [hygiene](https://rvwiki.mousetrap.net/doku.php?id=hygiene:bathing) and [toilet](https://rvwiki.mousetrap.net/doku.php?id=lifestyle:faq_toilet) # fedi resources - [vanlife community](/c/vanlife@lemmy.ml) - [national parks community](/c/nationalparks@lemmy.world) - [overlanding](/c/overlanding@lemmy.world) # rules of engagement - be civil - use descriptive thread titles. Pro tip: Help or Question are _not_ descriptive titles. - old-timers: assume people are different and have different needs, preferences, budgets, and use cases - newbies: demonstrate effort and willingness to learn; you'll need it on the road anyhow. Links have been added below to help get you started. When asking question state your use case (what you are trying to accomplish); we are terrible guessers. - tire-kickers: yes, we've heard that in a van down by the river joke. It was hi-larious in 1993. ----- vandwellers vandwelling vanlife urbancarliving

Mac Gaming

Welcome to the Mac Gaming Community! This is a place for all Mac gamers to discuss, share, and enjoy the world of gaming on macOS. Whether you're a casual player or a dedicated enthusiast, we welcome you to join our community and share your passion for Mac gaming! Rules Be respectful and civil. Treat others how you'd like to be treated. Personal attacks, harassment, and hate speech will not be tolerated. Stay on topic. All posts and discussions should be related to Mac gaming. Off-topic content may be removed. No piracy or illegal content. Do not share or promote pirated games, software, or any other illegal content. No self-promotion or spam. Keep the community clean by not sharing your own or others' videos, streams, or websites excessively. Use spoiler tags when necessary. Be considerate and use spoiler tags for content that could ruin the experience for others. Follow Lemmy's guidelines. Abide by the Lemmy Community Guidelines at all times.
