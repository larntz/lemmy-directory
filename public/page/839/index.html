Showerthoughts
Friday, June 16, 2023

A subreddit for sharing those miniature epiphanies you have that highlight the oddities within the familiar. Showerthought is a loose term that applies to the types of thoughts you might have while carrying out a routine task like showering, driving, or daydreaming. At their best, showerthoughts are universally relatable and find the amusing/interesting within the mundane. Please be respectful of others' submissions. Rudeness is unacceptable.
