Parabola community
Friday, June 16, 2023

### About You've reached the Parabola GNU/Linux-libre community in Lemmy. The Parabola project is a community-driven, labour-of-love effort to maintain a 100% free ([as in: freedom](https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw)) operating system distribution that is lean, clean, and hackable. Based on the Arch distribution, Parabola is a complete, user-friendly operating system, suitable for general everyday use, while retaining Arch's power-user charm. Parabola adheres to the [GNU Free System Distribution Guidelines (FSDG)](https://www.gnu.org/distros/free-system-distribution-guidelines.html). ### Documentation
