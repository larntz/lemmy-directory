For discussion of The Caretaker (AKA Leyland Kirby, The Stranger, and V/Vm) and other similar artists. **Rules** 1. Be respectful to everyone here. Obviously this includes basic criteria. Racism/homophobia/tranphobia/other types of bigotry will not be tolerated here, and will be met with an instant ban. In addition to that, we ask you to be respectful to those who are suffering with dementia or those who know someone going through something similar. We understand joking can be a coping mechanism for some, but it's not for everyone. 2. No NSFW 3.
