PCem
Thursday, June 15, 2023

This is Lemmy's first PCem community. 86Box discussion are allowed! From emulation.gametechwiki.com: PCem (an acronym for Personal Computer emulator) is an open-source, low-level PC emulator developed for Windows and Linux by Sarah Walker. It focuses on PC hardware from the 1980's and 1990's. A variety of operating systems can be installed in the emulator, which can then be used to launch programs. Official PCem website: pcem-emulator.co.uk PCem forum: https://pcem-emulator.co.uk/phpBB3
