Be good to each other. This should go without saying but please post quality content, treat other users with respect, and above all else just be kind. It's possible that the other user may not understand the material at first--every new interaction is a learning opportunity. 2. This is not the community for fictional horror stories/tales. Please only post genuine experiences, experiments, trials, questions, etc. 3. As a result of Rule #2, please assume that the stories you read here are truth and not falsehood. 4. Don't spam. ### NOTE The following has been extracted verbatim from the original sidebar of /r/DimensionalJumping: There is no established theory of 'jumping' or its mechanism, although there are numerous ways of viewing its nature.
